About Us

At Stemed, we are on a mission to ignite young minds with the power of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM). We conduct interactive workshops for students in grades 7 to 12, providing hands-on experience with Arduino, ESP32, sensors, Bluetooth-controlled projects, mobile app development, and simple home automation prototypes. Our goal is to make learning fun, practical, and engaging, empowering students with skills that go beyond textbooks.
