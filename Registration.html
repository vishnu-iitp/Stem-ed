7-days workshop syllabus

Learning Objectives

By the end of the workshop, participants will be able to:
1.  Understand foundational concepts in robotics and electronics.
2.  Program ESP32 microcontrollers for practical applications.
3.  Control motors, servos, and relays using drivers and PWM.
4.  Implement Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) and Wi-Fi connectivity.
5.  Design, build, and troubleshoot a Bluetooth-controlled robotic car.
6.  Create a smart switch system and connect it to Google Home or Alexa.
